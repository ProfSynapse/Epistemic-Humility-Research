## Verified findings (PDF read 2026-06-10)

- Abstract claim: "synthetic data generated by LLMs outperforms crowdsourced
  data in reducing hallucination in finetuned models" (retrieval-augmented
  product QA; Samsung Smart TV manual domain).
- Self-training (own outputs) ~= GPT-4o distillation for hallucination
  reduction; exposure-bias explanation.
- Also trains contextualized "I don't know" responses for unanswerable/
  retrieval-failure cases.
- Scope caveat for draft: single narrow RAG domain; directional counterpoint
  to Zhu 2311.13240, not a general refutation.
