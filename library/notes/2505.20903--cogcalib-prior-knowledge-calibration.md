## Verified findings (PDF read 2026-06-10)

- Mechanism: fine-tuning data aligned with the model's PRIOR KNOWLEDGE induces
  overconfidence; novel/unknown data improves calibration. The complement of
  Gekhman 2405.05904 (unknowns -> hallucination): knowns -> overconfidence.
- CogCalib (cognition-aware fine-tuning): avg 57% ECE reduction vs standard
  fine-tuning on Llama3-8B; 7 tasks, 3 model families; OOD generalization.
- Role in draft: C1 data-composition nuance. Reframes Zhu 2311.13240's 2023
  synthetic-vs-human contrast as known-vs-novel relative to the model.
